ECONOMICAL COMPACT NON-ELECTRICAL WASHING MACHINE PROJECT (USER CENTRED DESIGN 2)

  • January - April 2013
  • Team of four

Worked with Mr. Coen, a design researcher from Holland, for his project on developing a low-cost compact washing machine that runs without electricity. The user group targeted was low-income households in India who have space as well as electricity problems. Designed a questionnaire and conducted user survey and analysis from field visits to a number of low budget households. This analysis was used in the development of the washing machine, Hyper Wash.
